Learn with a Mini‑Story

1) 🌟 Meet Riya & Robo 🤖

Riya runs to Robo after school and says:
“Guess what, Robo! I scored the winning goal in football today. It was awesome!”
Robo’s eyes light up. “Awesome means something super good, right? No wonder you’re so happy!”
Riya nods, grinning: “Yep, awesome is my favorite happy word!”

The word awesome is a happy word.

2) Words carry feelings

Happy words (love, great) add
+ Positive
Sad words (bad, hate) add
− Negative
Neutral words (calm, bored) add
Neutral

3) Tricky bits

Not can flip meaning: “not good” feels negative. “Very” makes feelings stronger: “very happy”.
